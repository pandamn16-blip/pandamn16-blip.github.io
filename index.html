Why work with me

One person. Good taste. Real hands-on capability.

A lot of people can make a website look decent. Far fewer can also build the custom parts that make it properly useful.

Because I work hands-on and know how to code, I can help with both sides of the project — how it looks to your customers, and how it functions underneath.

That means fewer compromises, fewer handoffs, and more freedom to build what actually makes sense for your business instead of being limited by a standard setup.

  • Direct communicationYou deal with me from start to finish, not an account manager or a handoff halfway through.
  • Design + build in one placeThe visual side and the practical side are connected, which usually leads to a much better result.
  • Custom where it mattersIf your site needs something beyond the standard template, that is part of the job — not a problem.
  • Built around real needsI am comfortable tailoring things to suit your business and the way your customers actually use the site.
  • No agency dragYou are paying for useful work and sensible problem-solving, not layers of overhead.

Approach

Fair pricing. Sensible scope. Willing to go further when it matters.

I price projects based on the work involved and the value of what is being built — clearly and reasonably.

I also genuinely enjoy stretching a project further when there is a better way to do something, or when a more tailored solution would improve the end result.

So while the pricing should stay fair, the attitude is not minimal. I’m happy to go above and beyond to get something right — especially when that helps create a stronger website for you and helps me keep developing my own capability at the same time.

  • Clear, fixed pricing where possibleNo padded retainers or unnecessary complexity for the sake of it.
  • Paying for real workThe focus is on what is useful and what improves the result, not on inflating hours.
  • Flexible where it helpsIf there is a smarter or more tailored way to do something, I’m open to pushing the work further.
  • Built with careI want the finished site to genuinely work well for the business and the people using it.
